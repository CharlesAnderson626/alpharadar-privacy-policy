# Privacy Policy for AlphaRadar

**Last Updated: July 2026**

AlphaRadar ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data within the AlphaRadar Chrome extension.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
AlphaRadar does not collect, store, log, or track any of your personally identifiable information, browsing history, or user activity. All market data tracking occurs locally within your browser side panel.

### 2. Third-Party Services and Payments
We partner with ExtensionPay and Stripe to manage user accounts, subscriptions, and payment processing. 
* We do not personally collect, process, or store your credit card details, passwords, or emails. 
* Any authentication or payment data you provide is handled securely by ExtensionPay in accordance with their privacy guidelines.

### 3. Permissions Justification
AlphaRadar requests minimal browser permissions (`sidePanel`, `storage`, `alarms`, and `notifications`) purely to run the tracking dashboard, remember your free/paid preference state, and update market data smoothly.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted directly on this page.

### 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through our official GitHub repository channels.
